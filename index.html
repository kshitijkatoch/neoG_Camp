Biography

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi was an Indian lawyer, anti-colonial nationalist, and political ethicist who employed nonviolent resistance to lead the successful campaign for India's independence from British rule. He inspired movements for civil rights and freedom across the world.

Philosophy

Ahimsa(Nonvoilence)

Gandhi's nonviolent protests inspired many civil rights movements, including Martin Luther King Jr.'s.

He taught that nonviolence and truth can be applied in global affairs.

Satyagraha(Truth Force)

Gandhi's concept of "Satyagraha" emphazised the pursuit of truth and justice through non voilent means. It becaome a cornerstone in his struggle for India's Independence.

Legacy

Mahatma Gandhi's legacy includes a commitment to nonviolence, human rights, and independence. His ideas continue to inspire people around the world. He believed that to be a patriot, you didn't need to speak a certain language.
